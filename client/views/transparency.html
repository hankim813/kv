It seems to be increasingly difficult to find a company whose mission is an honorable cause. And then there was Everlane. A company whose mission is to serve customers their right.

As someone trying to be an individual in the midst of conformity, I find it most enjoyable to do so through my style of fashion. However, as not all companies have the same standard for products as Everlane, this can be quite a challenge.

The fashion industry was built by innovators but is now saturated by cheap replicators only looking for high returns. A uniquely designed and quality piece is reserved for the few who have the disposable income to afford it.

As a female looking to stamp her personality on the world with eye-catching textiles, there is little to choose from in the bargain bins. This is why I see Everlane as an idyllic brand in the fashion world.

You are the first step of one thousand more in changing the way business is implemented. In my career, I want to represent a brand whose meaning transcends its need for profit.
